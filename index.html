About

This webpage contains my submission for Statistics-2 Extra Activity 2. The objective of this activity is to perform a Monte Carlo Simulation in Python to verify a theoretical probability. The experiment was implemented in Google Colab and the results obtained through simulation closely match the theoretical value.

Extra Activity 2

Experiment

Roll two fair six-sided dice repeatedly. A player randomly chooses one of the following bets:

  • Under 7
  • Over 7
  • Exactly 7

A Monte Carlo Simulation with thousands of trials is used to estimate the expected gain and compare it with the theoretical expected gain.
